About audioartist

audioartist intends to combine art, technology and music by creating unique patterns based on a songs characteristics. Every song is unique in relation to their individual audio features, such as tempo, key and duration. audioartist attempts to display this by visually representating a song, creating unique pieces of art through these unique traits. No two pieces of art will ever be the same.

    This project is still a work in progress.
